I'm a process engineer. I spend my days applying multivariate statistics to pharmaceutical manufacturing. Then I go home and print things, brew coffee, and wonder if the same frameworks apply.

They do.

**In Control** is where I work through that — out loud, with math, and with graphics.

Each post follows a real project in real time. I don't have the answers before I start writing. That's the point.
